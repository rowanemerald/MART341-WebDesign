Crystal Carving-How It Works

By Wutong Crystal Carvings on Youtube


The newest of my collections!
My crystal carvings are 1 to 2 inches each.
They are each made with a different kind of gemstone.
All carved (supposedly) by hand with different size tiny dremels.

Here's my collection!


Toothess from How to Train Your Dragon! It was advertised as being made of fluorite,
HOWEVER, I recently found bubbles in it; and that means it's fake unfortunately...

Hello Kitty made from lepidolite! One of my favorite stones!


This fox is a conundrum. The seller discription said both quartz and white jade;
and obviously it can't be both those things.
It's a consistent white like a jade would be, but it's got some sparkle like a quartz might.
Then maybe glass? But it shows signs of being a real stone; like being heavy and cold.
So who knows!


Here's one of my favorites!
It a moon carved from aura citrine.
It's a complicated process to make something "aura".
A quartz crystal (which includes amethyst and citrine) is heat treated with a metal vapor,
like silver, using a vacuum chamber to make it show different colors from certain angles!


This wolf is made from labradorite!
Not in this one, but in other types of larbradorite, there is what's called a "flash".
A flash is a part of the stone that is shiny and iridescent with different colors!
Kind of like aura!
